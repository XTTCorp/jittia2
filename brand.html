Who is CompTIA?

We are inclusive
CompTIA believes every individual – regardless of their age, race, gender identity or sexual orientation deserves an equal chance for success and happiness.
  • We emphasize inclusion in our hiring, equity in our internal practices and work intentionally to eliminate bias in our training programs.
  • We strive to construct our platforms to be accessible – neither geographical location nor physical limitations should be a barrier to accessing and pursuing opportunity.
  • We actively look for chances to support people from populations that have historically been excluded from opportunities in tech.
We are results-focused
As an organization, CompTIA has a bias for action and results, all in the pursuit of our goals.  We constantly seek to stretch our own belief of what we are capable of accomplishing.
  • We constantly seek to stretch our own belief of what we are capable of accomplishing.
  • We have a results-focused and growth mentality, permeating everything we do in servicing our partners, customers, members and the industry.
  • We are driven to stay ahead of the waves in a fast-moving industry by designing and creating platforms and programs that deliver more value, usability and reach.
We are driven
At CompTIA, we believe great advances come from taking meaningful action through incremental bite-sized steps.
  • We are committed to being driven by excellence to create the best environment for success
  • We are flexible and open to new ideas.
  • We absorb all relevant information at each point along the journey to accomplishing our goals.
We are humble
With a higher volume of innovation, of course, comes greater risk.  We are humble enough to accept we can’t possibly know everything, nor can we say with complete certainty what the future might hold.
  • We are cautious and respectful.
  • We seek input through research, benchmark against the work of others and collaborate with others smarter than ourselves.
  • We believe a collective-informed consensus is an integral part of finding the best possible solution.
We are savvy
We believe in the intelligence, creativity, passion and purpose of the people who make up our global team, whose savvy keeps our engines running and gives us the credibility required to earn the repeat business of our partners, members and end users.
  • We wear our identity proudly.
  • We are confident that it is our people who will help us to fulfill our mission of unlocking potential of people working in, and aspiring to work in, the tech universe.
