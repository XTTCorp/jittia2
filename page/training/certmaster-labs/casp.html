Learn by Doing with CertMaster Labs for CASP+

CompTIA CertMaster Labs for CASP+ provides learners with the necessary platform to gain critical hands-on skills and develop a deeper understanding of the subject matter to prepare for your CompTIA CASP+ Certification. CertMaster Labs allows you to address the practical aspects of CASP+ exam objectives and complement prior training through access to real equipment and software environments.

BUY CERTMASTER LABS FOR CASP+    

Graded Hands-on Practice with CertMaster Labs for CASP+ (CSO-004)

All lab activities within the CASP+ course include gradable assessments, offer feedback and hints, and provide a score based on learner inputs, providing an accurate assessment of a learner’s ability to correctly and efficiently perform tasks. There are two types of labs in the course:

Assisted Labs guide the learner step-by-step through tasks, offering assessment and feedback throughout a 10-15 minute experience, allowing the learner to correct any misunderstandings as they proceed through the activity.

Applied Labs present a series of goal-oriented scenarios in a 20-30 minute experience covering multiple topics, scoring the learner’s work at the end of the activity based on their ability to successfully complete each scenario. As a result, learners are forced to think critically about how to approach problems without a prescribed set of steps

HOW YOU'LL GAIN HANDS-ON EXPERIENCE WITH CERTMASTER LABS FOR CASP+:

ACCESS TO HANDS-ON LEARNING

Access to equipment and software needed to gain critical real-world experience.

ALIGNED WITH EXAM OBJECTIVES

Lab exercises align real-world scenarios with the practical aspects of CompTIA exam objectives.

EXTENSIVE, STEP-BY-STEP LAB GUIDES

Instructions to navigate and accomplish lab tasks from basic configuration to advanced subjects.

WHAT YOU'LL LEARN WITH CERTMASTER LABS FOR CASP+

CertMaster Labs addresses the practical aspects of the exam objectives on the CompTIA CASP+ exam, including:

Security Architecture

Expanded coverage to analyze security requirements in hybrid networks to work toward an enterprise-wide, zero trust security architecture with advanced secure cloud and virtualization solutions.

Security Operations

Expanded emphasis on newer techniques addressing advanced threat management, vulnerability management, risk mitigation, incident response tactics, and digital forensics analysis.

Governance, Risk, and Compliance

Expanded to support advanced techniques to prove an organization’s overall cybersecurity resiliency metric and compliance to regulations, such as CMMC, PCI-DSS, SOX, HIPAA, GDPR, FISMA, NIST, and CCPA.

Security Engineering and Cryptography

Expanded to focus on advanced cybersecurity configurations for endpoint security controls, enterprise mobility, cloud/hybrid environments, and enterprise-wide PKI and cryptographic solutions.
